Movie Watchlist

Sci-Fi Comedy Drama Action Mystery Animation

The Fifth Element

In a dazzling, futuristic world, a cab driver and a mysterious woman must save humanity from an ancient evil—packed with wild action, quirky humor, and unforgettable sci-fi spectacle.

Problemista

A surreal, darkly comedic journey through the struggles of an aspiring toy designer from El Salvador, whose eccentric boss and ticking visa deadline turn the American Dream into a wild, absurdist adventure.

In the Mood for Love

A slow-burning, achingly beautiful story of two neighbors drawn together by longing and unspoken desire, set against the dreamy backdrop of 1960s Hong Kong.

Bad Boys

Explosive action and razor-sharp banter drive this high-energy buddy cop film, where two Miami detectives must take down a ruthless drug kingpin before their own tempers get the best of them.

Mystic River

A haunting crime drama where childhood trauma resurfaces in the wake of a murder, unraveling the bonds of three lifelong friends in devastating ways.

Spirited Away

A young girl stumbles into a mystical world of spirits and gods, where courage and friendship are her only hope of breaking free from an enchanting yet dangerous realm.

2001: A Space Odyessy

A mesmerizing journey through space and time, where human evolution collides with artificial intelligence, culminating in one of the most mysterious and thought-provoking endings in cinema history.

Clueless

A stylish, witty take on teen life in the ’90s, where a wealthy, self-absorbed high schooler hilariously learns that matchmaking and makeovers can’t fix everything.

Poetic Justice

With Janet Jackson’s heartfelt poetry and Tupac Shakur’s raw charisma, this romantic road trip through California’s streets is both tender and powerful.

Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon

A breathtaking mix of romance, mysticism, and gravity-defying martial arts, where legendary warriors chase a stolen sword and the ghosts of their past.

Misery

A best-selling author wakes up in the clutches of his "number one fan"—but as her obsession spirals into madness, survival becomes his deadliest plot twist yet.

Prince of Egypt

The epic story of Moses comes to life in breathtaking animation, as one man’s destiny leads him from Egyptian royalty to the liberator of his people.

Blade Runner

In a neon-soaked, dystopian future, a weary detective hunts down rogue androids—only to question what it truly means to be human.

Sister Act

A lounge singer on the run finds unexpected harmony—and comedy—when she hides out in a convent and turns a struggling choir into a soulful sensation.

Eyes Wide Shut

A hypnotic descent into desire and secrecy, as a man’s late-night journey into a secret society threatens to unravel his marriage—and his sanity.

Lara Croft: Tomb Raider

Armed with wit, athleticism, and twin pistols, the fearless Lara Croft races against time and ruthless enemies to recover an ancient artifact before it falls into the wrong hands.

Pulp Fiction

A pulsing, unpredictable ride through the underbelly of L.A., where hitmen, boxers, and gangsters cross paths in a darkly comedic and brutally stylish crime epic.

Perfect Blue

A psychological thriller that blurs the line between reality and illusion, as a former pop star’s descent into paranoia reveals a dark, twisted obsession lurking in the shadows.
